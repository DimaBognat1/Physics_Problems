# 10. Speed of Light in Media

## Given

We need to find the speed of light in diamond.

The index of refraction of diamond is:

n = 2.42

The speed of light in vacuum is:

c = 3.0 × 10^8 m/s

---

## Step 1: Formula

The speed of light in a medium is related to the speed of light in vacuum by the formula:

v = c / n

where:

- v is the speed of light in the medium
- c is the speed of light in vacuum
- n is the index of refraction

---

## Step 2: Substitute the values

v = (3.0 × 10^8) / 2.42

---

## Step 3: Calculate

v ≈ 1.24 × 10^8 m/s

---

## Explanation

The index of refraction shows how much slower light travels in a material compared with vacuum.

Since diamond has a relatively large refractive index, light travels much slower in diamond than in vacuum.

---

## Final Answer

The speed of light in diamond is:

v ≈ 1.24 × 10^8 m/s
