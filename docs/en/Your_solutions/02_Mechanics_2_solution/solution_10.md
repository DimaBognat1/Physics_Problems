# 10. Force Field and Power

We are given:

x = 5t² − t  
y = 2t³  
z = −3t + 2  

m = 0.5 kg  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Velocity

v = (dx/dt, dy/dt, dz/dt)  

v = (10t − 1, 6t², −3)  

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

a = (10, 12t, 0)  

---

### Step 3: Force

F = ma = 0.5 × a = (5, 6t, 0)  

---

### Step 4: Momentum

p = mv = (0.5(10t−1), 3t², −1.5)  

---

### Step 5: Power

P = F · v  

---

## Final Answer

v(t), a(t), F(t), p(t) as above  
P = F · v
