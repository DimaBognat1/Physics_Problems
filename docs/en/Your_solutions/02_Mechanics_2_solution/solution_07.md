# 7. Dynamics with Friction

We are given:

m₁ = 10 kg (moving block)  
m₂ = 5 kg (on top, tied)  
F = 45 N  
μ = 0.2  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Friction between blocks

Normal force:

N₁ = m₂g = 5 × 9.81 ≈ 49 N  

Friction:

f₁ = μN₁ ≈ 0.2 × 49 ≈ 9.8 N  

---

### Step 2: Friction with ground

Total normal:

N₂ = (m₁ + m₂)g = 15 × 9.81 ≈ 147 N  

f₂ = μN₂ ≈ 0.2 × 147 ≈ 29.4 N  

---

### Step 3: Net force

F_net = 45 − 9.8 − 29.4 ≈ 5.8 N  

---

### Step 4: Acceleration

a = F_net / m₁ ≈ 5.8 / 10 ≈ 0.58 m/s²  

---

## Final Answer

a ≈ 0.58 m/s²
