# 9. Vertical Throw with Drag

We are given:

m dv/dt = −mg − kv  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Solve differential equation

v(t) = (v₀ + mg/k)e^(−kt/m) − mg/k  

---

### Step 2: Maximum height

Occurs when v = 0:

0 = (v₀ + mg/k)e^(−kt/m) − mg/k  

Solve for t → substitute into x(t)

---

### Step 3: Comparison

With drag → lower maximum height than without drag  

---

## Final Answer

v(t) = (v₀ + mg/k)e^(−kt/m) − mg/k  

Height is smaller than in no-drag case
