# 12. Work and Energy with a Constant Force

We are given:

F = (6, 2), m = 2  

v(0) = (1, −1)  
r(0) = (0, 0)  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Acceleration

a = F/m = (3, 1)  

---

### Step 2: Velocity

v(t) = (3t+1, t−1)  

---

### Step 3: Position

r(t) = (1.5t² + t, 0.5t² − t)  

---

### Step 4: Work

W = F · r(t)  

---

## Final Answer

a(t), v(t), r(t) as above  
W = F · r
