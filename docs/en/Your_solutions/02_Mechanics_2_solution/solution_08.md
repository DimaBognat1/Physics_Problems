# 8. Work of a Variable Force

We are given:

F(x) = −kx  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Equation of motion

m d²x/dt² = −kx  

General solution:

x(t) = A cos(ωt) + B sin(ωt),  
where ω = √(k/m)

---

### Step 2: Work

W = ∫F dx = ∫(−kx) dx  

W = −(k/2)x₀²  

---

### Step 3: Potential energy

U(x) = (1/2)kx²  

Check:

F = −dU/dx  

---

## Final Answer

x(t) = A cos(ωt) + B sin(ωt)  
W = −(k/2)x₀²  
U = (1/2)kx²
