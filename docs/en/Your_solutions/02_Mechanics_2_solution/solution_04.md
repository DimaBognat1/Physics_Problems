# 4. Energy & Momentum

We are given:

m₁ = 0.5 kg  
h = 3.0 m  
m₂ = 1.5 kg (at rest)  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Velocity before collision

The block slides without friction, so mechanical energy is conserved:

mgh = (1/2)mv²  

v₁ = √(2gh)  

v₁ = √(2 × 9.81 × 3) ≈ 7.67 m/s  

---

### Step 2: Collision (perfectly inelastic)

After collision, the blocks stick together → momentum is conserved:

m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = (m₁ + m₂)v  

Since m₂ is at rest:

m₁v₁ = (m₁ + m₂)v  

v = (m₁v₁) / (m₁ + m₂)  

v = (0.5 × 7.67) / (0.5 + 1.5)  

v ≈ 3.835 / 2 ≈ 1.92 m/s  

---

## Final Answer

v ≈ 1.92 m/s
