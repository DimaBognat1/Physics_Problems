# 11. Time-Dependent Force

We are given:

F = (15t, 3t−12, −6t²), m = 3  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Acceleration

a = F/m = (5t, t−4, −2t²)  

---

### Step 2: Velocity

Integrate:

v(t) = (2.5t² + 2, 0.5t² − 4t, −(2/3)t³ + 1)  

---

### Step 3: Position

r(t) = (5 + (2.5/3)t³ + 2t,  
        2 + (1/6)t³ − 2t²,  
        −3 − (1/6)t⁴ + t)  

---

## Final Answer

v(t), r(t) as above
