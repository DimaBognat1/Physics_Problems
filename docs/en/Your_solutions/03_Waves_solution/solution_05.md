# 5. Echo Ranging

## Given

Speed of sound:

v = 343 m/s

Total time for echo:

t = 1 s

---

## Important idea

The sound travels twice:

1. from the person to the cliff  
2. from the cliff back to the person  

So the total time (1 s) includes both directions.

Therefore, one-way time is:

t_one-way = 1 / 2 = 0.5 s

---

## Step 1: Use distance formula

d = v × t

Substitute values:

d = 343 × 0.5

d = 171.5 m

---

## Explanation

If we used the full 1 second, we would calculate the total path (there and back).  
But we only need the distance to the cliff, so we divide time by 2.

---

## Final Answer

The distance to the cliff is:

d = 171.5 m
