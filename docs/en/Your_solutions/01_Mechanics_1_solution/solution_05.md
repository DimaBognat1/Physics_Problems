# Section 1 — Relative Velocity

Given:

River speed = 2 m/s (east)  
Boat speed = 5 m/s (relative to water)  
Width = 200 m  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Direction

To go straight north, the boat must cancel river flow:

sinθ = 2 / 5  

θ = arcsin(0.4) ≈ 23.6°  

So the boat aims 23.6° west of north.

---

### Step 2: Effective velocity across river

v = 5 cosθ  

cosθ ≈ √(1 − 0.4²) ≈ 0.916  

v ≈ 5 × 0.916 ≈ 4.58 m/s  

---

### Step 3: Time

t = 200 / 4.58 ≈ 43.7 s  

---

## Final Answer

Direction: ≈ 23.6° west of north  
Time: ≈ 43.7 s
