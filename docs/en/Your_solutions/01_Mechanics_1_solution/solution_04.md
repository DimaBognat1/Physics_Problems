# Section 1 — Vector Calculus

We are given:

r(t) = (3t²)i + (5t − 8t²)j  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Velocity

v(t) = dr/dt  

v(t) = (6t)i + (5 − 16t)j  

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

a(t) = dv/dt  

a(t) = (6)i − (16)j  

---

## Final Answer

v(t) = (6t, 5 − 16t)  
a(t) = (6, −16)
