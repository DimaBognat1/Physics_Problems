# Section 1 — Momentum Comparison

We are given:

Fly: m = 2 g = 0.002 kg, v = 10 m/s  
Ball: m = 60 g = 0.06 kg, v = 1 m/s  

---

## Solution

Momentum:

p = mv  

Fly:

p₁ = 0.002 × 10 = 0.02 kg·m/s  

Ball:

p₂ = 0.06 × 1 = 0.06 kg·m/s  

---

## Final Answer

The tennis ball has greater momentum.
