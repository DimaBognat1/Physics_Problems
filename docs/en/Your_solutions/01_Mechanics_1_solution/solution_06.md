# Section 1 — Variable Velocity

We are given:

v(t) = t² + 2t − 5  
x(0) = 4  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Position

x(t) = ∫v(t)dt  

x(t) = (1/3)t³ + t² − 5t + C  

Use initial condition:

4 = 0 + 0 − 0 + C  

C = 4  

So:

x(t) = (1/3)t³ + t² − 5t + 4  

---

### Step 2: Acceleration

a(t) = dv/dt  

a(t) = 2t + 2  

---

### Step 3: Values at t = 3

x(3) = (1/3)(27) + 9 − 15 + 4  

= 9 + 9 − 15 + 4 = 7  

a(3) = 2(3) + 2 = 8  

---

## Final Answer

x(3) = 7  
a(3) = 8
