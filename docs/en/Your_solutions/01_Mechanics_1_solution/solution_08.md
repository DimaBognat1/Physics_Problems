# Section 1 — Circular Motion

We are given:

Earth radius R = 6378 km = 6.378 × 10⁶ m  
Period T = 24 h = 86400 s  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Formula

Centripetal acceleration:

a = ω²R  

where ω = 2π / T  

---

### Step 2: Angular velocity

ω = 2π / 86400 ≈ 7.27 × 10⁻⁵ rad/s  

---

### Step 3: Acceleration

a = ω²R  

a ≈ (7.27 × 10⁻⁵)² × 6.378 × 10⁶  

a ≈ 0.034 m/s²  

---

## Final Answer

a ≈ 0.034 m/s²
