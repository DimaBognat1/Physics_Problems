# Section 0 — Systems of Equations

We are given the system:

2x + 3y = 12  
x − y = 1  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Express one variable

From the second equation:

x = y + 1  

---

### Step 2: Substitute into the first equation

2(y + 1) + 3y = 12  

2y + 2 + 3y = 12  

5y + 2 = 12  

---

### Step 3: Solve for y

5y = 10  

y = 2  

---

### Step 4: Find x

x = y + 1 = 2 + 1 = 3  

---

## Final Answer

x = 3  
y = 2
