# Section 0 — Logic & Series

A bicycle is 10 meters from a wall and moves toward it at 1 m/s.  
A fly moves at 2 m/s between the bicycle and the wall.

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Time until bicycle reaches the wall

t = distance / speed = 10 / 1 = 10 s  

---

### Step 2: Distance traveled by the fly

The fly moves continuously for 10 seconds at 2 m/s:

distance = speed × time = 2 × 10 = 20 m  

---

## Final Answer

The fly travels 20 meters.
