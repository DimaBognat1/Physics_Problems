# Section 0 — Definite Integrals

We need to compute:

∫₀^π sin(x) dx  

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Find antiderivative

∫ sin(x) dx = −cos(x)  

---

### Step 2: Apply limits

= [−cos(x)]₀^π  

= (−cosπ) − (−cos0)  

= −(−1) + 1  

= 1 + 1 = 2  

---

## Final Answer

The area is 2.
