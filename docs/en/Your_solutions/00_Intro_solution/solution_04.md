# Section 0 — Rearranging Formulas

We are given the formula:

T = 2π √(l / g)

We need to express g.

---

## Solution

### Step 1: Divide both sides by 2π

T / (2π) = √(l / g)

---

### Step 2: Square both sides

(T / (2π))² = l / g

---

### Step 3: Rearrange for g

g = l / (T / (2π))²

---

### Step 4: Simplify

g = l * (2π / T)²  

g = 4π²l / T²  

---

## Final Answer

g = 4π²l / T²
